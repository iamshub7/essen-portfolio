Essen Technologies: Advancing Precision in Industry

At Essen Technologies, we take great pride in our role as an authorized Value Added Reseller (VAR) for METTLER TOLEDO in the territories as Pune, Ahilyanagar, Satara, Sangli, Kolhapur & Solapur districts. Our dedication to providing exceptional sales, service, and spare parts reflects our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Partnering with METTLER TOLEDO INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, a subsidiary of Mettler-Toledo International, allows us to leverage the largest installation base of weighing and lab instruments in the world, offering our clients access to state-of-the-art technology and unparalleled expertise.

  • Industrial Division

    Dust proof & Wash Proof - Weighing scale, Truck weigh bridge, Tank weighing system, Parts counting scale, Recipe Weighing system

  • Laboratary Divsion

    Analytical balances, Moisture Analyzer, UV_VIS Spectrophotometer, KF Titrator, pH Meter, Density Meter, Refractometer

At Essen Technologies, we understand that our greatest value lies in our ability to integrate these sophisticated instruments into your existing workflows. By effectively combining our technology with your processes, we enable you to optimize efficiency, reduce operational errors, and improve overall performance.With more than 25 years of experience in the industry, our team brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every client engagement. We hold valid licenses issued by Legal Metrology, ensuring that our offerings comply with all regulatory standards. This experience allows us to navigate the complexities of various applications and provide you with personalized support that meets your specific needs. At Essen Technologies, we are committed to delivering a diverse portfolio of high-quality weighing and lab products. Whether you are looking to enhance product quality, streamline operations, or ensure compliance, you can trust us to be your dedicated partner in precision and excellence. Together, we can drive innovation and success in your operations, ensuring that you remain at the forefront of your industry.

From the Director's Desk

Deepak Janardan Shinde(D.E.R.E)

Founder of ESSEN Technologies, a company focused on delivering reliable industrial measurement solutions and service excellence across diverse industries. He began his progressing professional journey in April 1994 with Essae-Teraoka as a Trainee Engineer to Unit Head of a branch, building strong technical expertise in front line sales and team management. In 2002, joined Mettler-Toledo, where he spent over a decade contributing to regional business growth, customer service operations and technical support leadership. Through years of hands-on experience and industry exposure, he developed deep insight into industrial measurement systems and customer-centric service management. In 2013, he founded ESSEN Technologies with a vision to create a trusted organization focused on brand selling with efficient service, and long-term customer partnerships. Under his leadership, the company continues to support industries with the most reliable solutions and strong after-sales service capabilities.
